Item 2.01 Completion of Acquisition or Disposition of Assets
On December 1, 2018, Flagstar Bank, FSB (the Bank), a wholly-owned bank subsidiary of Flagstar Bancorp, Inc. (the Company), closed its previously announced transaction pursuant to the Purchase and Assumption Agreement with Wells Fargo Bank, N.A. (Wells Fargo), to acquire certain assets and assume certain liabilities of 52 Wells Fargo branches (the Wells Fargo Branches) in Indiana, Michigan, Wisconsin and Ohio for a deposit premium of 7%. The Bank has assumed approximately $2 billion of deposit liabilities and has acquired approximately $115 million in loans related to the Wells Fargo Branches.
