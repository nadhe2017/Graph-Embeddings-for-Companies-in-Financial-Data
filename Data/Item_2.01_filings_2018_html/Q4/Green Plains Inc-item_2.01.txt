Item 2.01
Completion of Acquisition or Disposition of Assets.

As previously announced, on October 23, 2018, Green Plains and Green Plains II LLC (GP II), an indirect wholly-owned subsidiary of Green Plains, entered into a stock purchase agreement with Kerry Holding Co. (Kerry) for GP II to sell all of the issued and outstanding capital stock of Fleischmanns Vinegar Company, Inc. (the Kerry Transaction). On November 27, 2018, Green Plains closed on the Kerry Transaction. Green Plains received as consideration from Kerry approximately $353.9 million in cash and restricted cash, including net working capital adjustments.
