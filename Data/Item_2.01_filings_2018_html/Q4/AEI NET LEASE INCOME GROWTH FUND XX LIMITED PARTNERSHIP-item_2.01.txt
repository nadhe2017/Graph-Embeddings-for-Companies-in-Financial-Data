Item 2.01 Completion of Acquisition or Disposition of Assets.
On October 30, 2018, the Partnership sold a Red Robin restaurant in Colorado Springs, Colorado to L.A.E. Properties, Inc., an unrelated third party. The Partnership received net cash proceeds of approximately $5,517,000 for the property, which resulted in a net gain of approximately $4,344,500.
