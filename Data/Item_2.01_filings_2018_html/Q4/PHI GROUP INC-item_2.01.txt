ITEM 2.01 COMPLETION OF ACQUISITION OR DISPOSITION OF ASSETS.
On September 28, 2018 PHI Group, Inc. entered into a Closing Memorandum to close the acquisition of a 51% equity interest in Vinafilms Joint Stock Company, a Vietnamese company, and delivered 50 million shares of the Companys Class A Series III Cumulative Convertible Redeemable Preferred Stock to the majority shareholder of Vinafilms.
