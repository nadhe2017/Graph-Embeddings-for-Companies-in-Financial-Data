Item 2.01.
Completion of Acquisition or Disposition of Assets.
As previously disclosed on Form 8-K, on March 1, 2018, Microchip, Microsemi Corporation, a Delaware corporation (Microsemi), Maple Acquisition Corporation, a Delaware corporation and wholly owned subsidiary of Microchip (Merger Sub), entered into an Agreement and Plan of Merger (the Merger Agreement). This Form 8-K is being filed in connection with the consummation of the transactions contemplated by the Merger Agreement.
