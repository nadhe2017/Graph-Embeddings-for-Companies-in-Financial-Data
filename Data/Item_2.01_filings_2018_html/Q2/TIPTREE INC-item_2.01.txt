Item 2.01
Completion of Acquisition or Disposition of Assets.
On April 10, 2018, Tiptree completed the Reorganization Merger and issued to the former limited partners of TFP the Merger Consideration and canceled all outstanding Class B common stock of Tiptree. Upon the issuance of the Merger Consideration, Tiptree has 37,933,595 shares of Class A common stock outstanding (excluding 5,118,438 shares of Class A common stock held by a subsidiary of Tiptree). Effective April 10, 2018, Tiptree owns 100% of OpCo and Tiptrees Class A common stock is the only outstanding class of stock of Tiptree.
