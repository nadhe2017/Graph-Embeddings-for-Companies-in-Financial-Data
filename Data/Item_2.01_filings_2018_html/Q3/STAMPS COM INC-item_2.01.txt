Item 2.01
Completion of Acquisition or Disposition of Assets.
On August 15, 2018, Stamps.com Inc., a Delaware corporation (SDC), through its wholly owned subsidiary Pacific Shelf 1855 Limited (Pacific Shelf), completed the acquisition (the Acquisition) of MetaPack Limited, a private limited company incorporated in England and Wales (MetaPack), pursuant to a share purchase agreement dated July 24, 2018, as amended (the Agreement), by and among the certain key sellers named in the Agreement (the Key Sellers), MetaPack, Pacific Shelf and SDC as Pacific Shelfs guarantor.
Pursuant to the Agreement and a related agreement to purchase Minority Shares (as defined below), Pacific Shelf acquired 100% of MetaPacks issued and to be issued share capital by purchasing (i) all of the Key Sellers shares of MetaPack, representing approximately 80% of the total outstanding shares and (ii) all other issued and to be issued shares of MetaPack (Minority Shares), for an aggregate purchase price, for all such shares, of approximately One Hundred and Seventy Five Million GBP (175,000,000). After adjustment for certain transaction-related costs such as debt and professional fees, and accounting for certain share option-related amounts and taxes, the resulting aggregate cash payment for all of the shares was approximately One Hundred and Seventy One Million GBP (171,000,000) (about $220 million at current exchange rates).
