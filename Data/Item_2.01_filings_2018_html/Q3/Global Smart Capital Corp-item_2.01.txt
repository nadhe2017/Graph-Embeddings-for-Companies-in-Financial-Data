Item 2.01 Completion of Acquisition or Disposition of Assets

Pursuant to the agreement, the Company will acquire 100% of the issued and outstanding shares in Global Small and Medium Sized Enterprise Incubation Listed Promotion Association Limited (GSMA), a Hong Kong registered Corporation.
