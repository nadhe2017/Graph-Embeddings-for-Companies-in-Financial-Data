Item 2.01 Completion of Acquisition or Disposition of Assets.
On September 12, 2018, pursuant to the Agreement and Plan of Merger (the Merger Agreement), by and among the Company, Flash Merger Sub Corp., a Delaware corporation and a wholly owned subsidiary of the Company (Merger Sub), Grakon Parent, Inc., a Delaware corporation (Grakon), and Industrial Growth Partners IV, L.P., a Delaware limited partnership, the Company completed the acquisition of Grakon (the Grakon Acquisition). Pursuant to the Merger Agreement, Merger Sub merged with and into Grakon with Grakon as the surviving entity in such merger and as a result of which Grakon became a direct wholly owned subsidiary of the Company. The Company paid a purchase price of $420 million in cash, on a cash-free, debt-free basis, subject to customary purchase price adjustments.
