ITEM 2.01
Completion of Acquisition or Disposition of Assets.
On July 31, 2018, Myriad Genetics, Inc. (Myriad) completed its acquisition of Counsyl, Inc. (Counsyl), in accordance with the terms of the previously announced Agreement and Plan of Merger (Merger Agreement), dated May 25, 2018, by and among Myriad, Cinnamon Merger Sub, Inc., a wholly owned subsidiary of Myriad (Merger Subsidiary), Counsyl and Fortis Advisors LLC, as the representative of the securityholders of Counsyl. Pursuant to the terms of the Merger Agreement, Merger Subsidiary was merged with and into Counsyl, with Counsyl continuing as the surviving corporation and wholly owned subsidiary of Myriad (the Merger).
