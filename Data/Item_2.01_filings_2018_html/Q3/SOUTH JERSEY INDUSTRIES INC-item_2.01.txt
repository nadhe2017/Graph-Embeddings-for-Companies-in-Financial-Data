Item 2.01
Completion of Acquisition or Disposition of Assets.
On July 1, 2018, the Company completed the previously announced acquisition of the Elizabethtown Business and the Elkton Business pursuant to the terms of the Elizabethtown Purchase Agreement and the Elkton Purchase Agreement, respectively. The Company paid $1.69 billion in cash for the Elizabethtown Business and $10 million in cash for the Elkton Business on July 1, 2018, plus an adjustment for the net working capital of the Elizabethtown Business and Elkton Business.
