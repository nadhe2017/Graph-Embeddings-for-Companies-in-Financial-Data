ITEM 2.01
Completion of Acquisition or Disposition of Assets.
On June 30, 2018, iStar Inc. (the "Company") obtained effective control over iStar Net Lease I LLC (the "Net Lease Venture"). As a result, effective June 30, 2018, the Company will consolidate the Net Lease Venture which had previously been accounted for as an equity method investment. For additional information on the Net Lease Venture, see Note 7 in the Company's Quarterly Report on Form 10-Q for the period ended March 31, 2018.
