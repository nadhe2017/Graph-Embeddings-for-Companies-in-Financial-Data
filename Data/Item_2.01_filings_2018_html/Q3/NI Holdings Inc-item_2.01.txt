Item 2.01.
Completion of Acquisition or Disposition of Assets.
On August 31, 2018, NI Holdings, Inc. (NI Holdings) completed the previously announced acquisition of all outstanding shares of capital stock of Direct Auto Insurance Company (Direct Auto). The acquisition closed pursuant to a stock purchase agreement (the Stock Purchase Agreement) dated May 31, 2018. The purchase price paid by NI Holdings was $17 million in cash.
The State of Illinois Department of Insurance approved the acquisition on August 27, 2018.
The foregoing description of the acquisition and the Stock Purchase Agreement is a summary, does not purport to be a complete description, and is qualified in its entirety by reference to the full text of the Stock Purchase Agreement, which is incorporated by reference as Exhibit 2.1.
