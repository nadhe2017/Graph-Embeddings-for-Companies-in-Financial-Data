Item 2.01 Completion of Acquisition or Disposition of Assets
On August 1, 2018, Argentum FM completed the acquisition of Cheshire Trafford, as described in Item 1.01, above. Argentum FM paid the initial tranche of U.S.$175,710.30 (132,361.83 GBP) to the owners Cheshire Trafford in exchange for 100% of the ordinary shares of Cheshire Trafford.
As a result of such acquisition, Argentum FM now has approximately U.S.$38.75 million (29.2 million GBP) of funds under administration.
The Company will file financial statements and pro forma financial information on Cheshire Trafford in an amendment to this Current Report on Form 8-K within 71 days of the date of this filing.
