Item 2.01 Completion of Acquisition or Disposition of Assets.
On the Closing Date, pursuant to the Merger Agreement, the Company merged with TMG. The Company is the surviving corporation. Each shareholder of TMG received one (1) share of common stock of the Company for every one (1) share of TMG held as of August 16, 2018. In accordance with the terms of the merger agreement, all of the shares of TMG held by TMG shareholders were cancelled, and 40,000,002 shares of common stock of the Company will be issued to the TMG shareholders. Following the Merger, the Company has 40,777,471 shares of common stock issued and outstanding.
