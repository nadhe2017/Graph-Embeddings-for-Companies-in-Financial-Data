Item 2.01 Completion of Acquisition of Disposition of Asset.
On
July 27, 2018
, the Company completed the sale of our Peninsula Office Park property (the "Property"), a 447,739-square-foot office property located in San Mateo, California, for a gross sale price of $210.0 million (before certain credits, prorations and closing costs). The Property was sold to Harvest Properties, Inc., with whom the Company has no other relationship.
