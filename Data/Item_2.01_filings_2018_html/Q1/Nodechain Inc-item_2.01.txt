Item 2.01 Completion of Acquisition or Disposition of Assets.
On January 2, 2018 we entered into and consummated an agreement to purchase 10,000 Ripple (XRP) ("Units") of cryptocurrency. The purchase is considered a related party transaction since the Seller of the units of Ripple, Mewe World, Inc., is controlled by our Chairman of the Board of Directors, Alham Benyameen.
